About

With the release of the Eevee render engine in Blender, a whole suite of new possibilities was unlocked. I often experiented with the new features and the new render engine, which allows users to work with rendered models in real-time. Eevee also allows the user to enable post-processing effects such as bloom and ambient occlusion, but in real-time. At the time, I had an assignment to create a cyberpunk style city in a group, so I took the opportunity to test out how neon assets would look. The result was what you see above. A mix of text, shapes and line art with emission materials makes for a super easy, super fast neon effect that looks great.

This was one of the more fun experiments I tried with Eevee.
